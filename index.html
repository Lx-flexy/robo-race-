DETAILED RULES
🔧 BUILD RULES
▼
  • No ready-made chassis — robot must be self-built
  • Allowed materials: MDF, foam, cardboard, acrylic, 3D printing, etc.
  • No modification allowed after technical check
  • Robot must be safe and non-destructive to the arena
📡 CONTROL & COMMUNICATION
▼
  • Only wireless control is allowed: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa, Zigbee, RF, etc.
  • Wired control is strictly prohibited
  • On-board power supply only — no external power source
  • Max voltage: 12V
💻 TECHNOLOGY & COMPONENTS
▼
  • Allowed controllers: Arduino, ESP, STM, Raspberry Pi, etc.
  • Any standard electronic components are permitted
  • All components must be properly secured within the robot frame
⚖️ GENERAL RULES
▼
  • No arena damage allowed — teams responsible for any damage caused
  • Judges' decision is final and binding
  • Any violation of rules leads to immediate disqualification
  • Team must report to the venue 30 minutes before their slot
  • College ID or registration proof is mandatory
